Bas being Bas

A true mixed martial arts (MMA) pioneer, UFC heavyweight champion, Hall of Famer, and three-time King Of Pancrase, Bas Rutten enjoyed a storied career in the sport before moving on to commentate for the legendary PRIDE organization in its heyday. He’s also renowned for being one of the most colorful, charismatic fighters ever to compete in either the ring or the cage.
